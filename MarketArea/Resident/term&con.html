Terms and Conditions

Welcome to the Barangay E-Blotter System. By accessing or using this system, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully.


Use of the System

The E-Blotter System is intended for residents to file and track complaints within the barangay. You must provide accurate and complete information when submitting a complaint. Misuse of the system, including submitting false or malicious complaints, is strictly prohibited.

Data Privacy

All personal information submitted will be kept confidential and used only for resolving complaints. Only authorized barangay personnel have access to complaint details.


User Responsibilities

You are responsible for the accuracy of the information you provide. You must not use the system for unlawful purposes or to harass others.


System Availability

The barangay strives to keep the system available at all times but does not guarantee uninterrupted access. Maintenance or technical issues may cause temporary downtime.


Changes to Terms

The barangay reserves the right to update these terms at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.


Contact

For questions or concerns regarding these terms, please contact the Barangay Office at (012) 345-6789 or email barangayeblotter@email.com.
